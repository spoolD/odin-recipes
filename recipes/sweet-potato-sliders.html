Sweet Potato Sliders

Description

Fun little sliders that make you feel better about yourself as they are bun free!

Ingredients

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 2 large sweet potatoes
  • 1 large avocado
  • Other toppings as desired

Steps

  1. Prehead the oven to 400F
  2. Peel and slice the sweet potatoes into approximately 1/4 inch thick slices. Coat with olive oil and season both sides
  3. Cook the sweet potatoes for 20 minutes, flip and cook for an additional 20 minutes
  4. While the sweet potatoes are cooking. Form the burger patties and season. Add to a hot pan and cook 2-3 minutes a side.
  5. Smash the avocado into a spread and season to taste
  6. Assemble the sliders with two sweet potato slices, patty, and avocado.
